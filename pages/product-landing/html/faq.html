FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Find answers to common questions about Dust//Protocol

What platforms will Dust//Protocol be available on?

Dust//Protocol will be available on PlayStation 5, Xbox Series X|S, and PC. The game is optimized to take full advantage of next-gen hardware.

When is the release date?

The game is scheduled for release in Q4 2084. Pre-orders are now available with exclusive bonuses for early adopters.

What are the system requirements for PC?

Minimum requirements will be announced closer to release. However, we recommend a high-end gaming PC with the latest neural interface compatibility for the best experience.

Will there be multiplayer features?

Yes! Dust//Protocol features both competitive and cooperative multiplayer modes, allowing players to hack together or compete in the digital realm.

What kind of cyberware upgrades are available?

Players can enhance their character with various cyberware upgrades, including neural implants, combat enhancements, and hacking tools. Each upgrade affects gameplay and character abilities.

Is there a character creation system?

Yes, players can fully customize their character's appearance, background, and starting attributes. Your choices will affect your starting position in the game world.

Will there be post-launch content?

Yes, we have planned regular updates including new missions, cyberware, and story expansions. Season Pass holders will receive all major content updates.

Can I play the game offline?

Yes, the main story campaign can be played entirely offline. However, some features like multiplayer modes and certain side activities require an internet connection.
